Matt

Matthew, along with Caycie, grew up in Logansport, Indiana where he attended Logansport schools and played a LOT of baseball (He's actually pretty much a pro). He also played soccer, but baseball was his passion - until he met Caycie. He's a die-hard Chicago Cubs fan, or as Caycie refers to them, the "Bad News Baby Bears", and he is a certified ninja. He attended Tri-State University where he became a brother of the Tau Kappa Epsilon Fraternity, and he loved that too - maybe even just a little too much. He is currently a website developer at CIK Enterprises in Indianapolis, and Caycie is so proud! He also loves Caycie's dog, Mischka, whom after the wedding will be hyphenating his name to Mischka Rance-Williams. Both Mischka nad Matthew are amazing cuddlers and the sweetest boys you will ever meet. P.S.- Matthew is not really a ninja, but Caycie still loves him just the same!

Caycie

Caycie is a 22-year-old, bonafide beauty! She attended Pioneer High School, and then graduated from the University of Indianapolis in 2013 with a degree in Exercise Science. That's right - she's a scientist! While she may never actually admit it, she is hands-down the most caring and generous person that anyone has ever known - and she isn't too tough on the eyes either! When she isn't busy hanging out with her "little", Haley, you can almost guarantee that she is doing something with the OTHER love of her life, Mischka. Having impeccable taste, Matt knew as soon as he met her that she was something special and worth holding onto. At first, distance was an issue, so Matt had to win her heart with sweet text messages and late-night phone calls. After he won her over, he decided to seal the deal (and subsequently hit the proverbial jackpot) by asking her to marry him while on the beach in one of the couple's favorite vacation spots, Myrtle Beach, SC. CHA-CHING!
